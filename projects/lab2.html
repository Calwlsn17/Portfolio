Electric Atlas: A Hendrix-Inspired Basemap

Mapbox Studio Styled Basemap

This project aims to bring back a creative and artistic element to cartography, which has become more focused on function over form ever since web mapping was introduced. The design for this basemap was inspired by Jimi Hendrix and 1960’s psychedelic art. Using bold colors, wavy textures, abstract symbols, and bubbly typefaces to embody the aesthetic of album covers and posters from the late 60’s and early 70’s, all while maintaining functionality. Parks and natural areas symbolized with the tree icon, while arts and music vanues are symbolized with the eye icon.
