Wisconsin Broadband Map

Static cartographic layout

This map shows where high-speed internet access is lacking across Wisconsin. It uses counties to show the bigger picture, with cell towers added to give a sense of where service actually exists. A small inset of Dane County zooms in on local differences that aren’t obvious at the statewide scale. The design is kept simple so the patterns are easy to see without getting distracted by extra detail.
